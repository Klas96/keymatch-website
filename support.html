Common questions

  • Reset your password: use "Forgot password" on the sign-in screen, or visit our password reset page.
  • Delete your account: open the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your profile and data are removed. You can also email us to request deletion.
  • Manage your subscription: Premium subscriptions are billed by the App Store. Manage or cancel them in your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Report a user or content: use the report option in the app, or email us at the address above with details.
